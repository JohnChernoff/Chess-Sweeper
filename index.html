Welcome to Sweeper Chess!

In each position there are some missing pieces that you must place on the board to solve the puzzle.
Add/reposition pieces with right/left clicking or dragging.
Numbers indicate the degree of control one side exerts over a square.
For instance, if two Black pieces and one White piece can move to a square, the number would be -1.
Numbers only appear on squares that have currently incorrect (compared to the solution) control values.
